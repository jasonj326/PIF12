## 🇺🇸 English Version

> **PIF12** is deployed on the Base network.
>
> Starting as a solo initiative looking for 12 partners in its first year, the vision is to evolve into a decentralized "Republic Labs" by the end of this 12-year journey.
>
> We deeply understand that everything we rely on daily is the result of countless people's labor. It is through mutual support that connections are forged, and from these connections, a true community and sense of belonging are born.
>
> Here, we find joy in helping others and feel no shame in asking for help. Even when we cannot repay a kindness directly, we transform that gratitude into help for someone else—paying it forward.

### ✨ Core Features

* 🛡️ **Soulbound Tokens (SBT):** Strictly non-transferable identity badges representing community membership and specific achievements (e.g., Year of the Horse Genesis Member).
* ⚖️ **Karma Ledger (Pay It Forward):** A signed integer (`int256`) ledger tracking moral debt and merit. Positive karma represents community contributions; negative karma represents karmic debt.
* 🤝 **Social Recovery:** A dual-guardian, time-locked recovery mechanism. Wallet recovery relies on human relationships rather than seed phrases.
* 🐺 Admin Safeguard & Lone-Wolf Mode: The ecosystem is overseen by a Default Admin (a multisig Safe) to provide an emergency safety net for account recovery. However, users who demand absolute self-sovereignty can activate "Lone-Wolf Mode" to permanently revoke admin access, achieving 100% decentralized ownership of their identity.

### ✨ 核心機制

* 🛡️ **靈魂綁定身分 (SBT):** 基於 ERC-1155 標準，絕對不可轉讓的數位徽章。代表社群成員身分與特定成就（例如：馬年創世成員徽章）。
* ⚖️ **鏈上陰德值帳本 (Karma):** 支援正負整數的信用評分系統。正數代表對社群的貢獻與善意，負數代表積欠的債務或求助。
* 🤝 **社交恢復機制 (Social Recovery):** 雙守護者、具備時間鎖的錢包救援機制。用戶若遺失私鑰，可透過其指定的兩位社群守護者轉移身分與陰德值。
* 🐺 管理員守護與孤狼模式 (Lone-Wolf Mode): 系統預設由最高管理員（Safe 多簽金庫）提供緊急的帳號救援防線。但追求絕對自治的使用者，可啟動「孤狼模式」永久註銷管理員的介入權限，達成 100% 真正去中心化的個人數位主權。
