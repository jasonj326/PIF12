🌌 PIF12 (Pay It Forward — 12-Year Legacy)English | 繁體中文<a name="english"></a>📖 OverviewPIF12 is a 12-year Web3 social experiment deployed on the Base network. It serves as the foundational Identity and Reputation Layer for a decentralized community, transforming social interactions into a verifiable, on-chain legacy.By combining non-transferable Soulbound Tokens (SBT) with a dynamic Karma ledger, PIF12 creates a trust-based ecosystem where good deeds are recorded indefinitely, and wallet recovery relies on human relationships rather than seed phrases.✨ Core FeaturesSoulbound Tokens (SBT): ERC-1155 based, strictly non-transferable identity badges representing community membership and specific achievements (e.g., Year of the Horse Genesis Member).Karma Ledger (Pay It Forward): A signed integer (int256) ledger tracking moral debt and merit. Positive karma represents community contributions; negative karma represents karmic debt.Social Recovery: A dual-guardian, time-locked recovery mechanism. If a user loses their private key, their designated guardians can migrate their SBT and Karma to a new wallet.The "Reincarnation Tax": A unique game-theory design. If a user with negative karma triggers Social Recovery, they suffer a 1.33x amplified penalty on their karmic debt, preventing users from escaping negative reputation.Gasless Transactions (ERC-2771): Native support for meta-transactions. Users can interact with the ecosystem seamlessly (e.g., via Privy + Line/Google SSO) without needing to pay or understand gas fees.🛠 Tech StackNetwork: Base (L2)Standard: ERC-1155Architecture: UUPS Upgradeable ProxyDependencies: OpenZeppelin Contracts📍 Contract AddressesNetwork: Base Sepolia (Testnet)Proxy Address: 0xA633AD478072f2C1216f552f710D72B691352bc4Status: V5 (Audit Reviewed)📄 LicenseThis project is licensed under the MIT License.<a name="繁體中文"></a>📖 專案介紹PIF12 是一個部署在 Base 鏈上的 12 年 Web3 社會實驗。它作為去中心化社群的「底層身分與聲譽協議」，將人際互動轉化為可驗證的鏈上資產。透過結合不可轉讓的靈魂綁定代幣 (SBT) 與動態的陰德值 (Karma) 帳本，PIF12 打造了一個基於信任的生態系。在這裡，善意將被永久記錄，而錢包的找回不再依賴冰冷的助記詞，而是依賴真實的人際守護網路。✨ 核心機制靈魂綁定身分 (SBT): 基於 ERC-1155 標準，絕對不可轉讓的數位徽章。代表社群成員身分與特定成就（例如：馬年創世成員徽章）。鏈上陰德值帳本 (Karma): 支援正負整數的信用評分系統。正數代表對社群的貢獻與善意，負數代表積欠的債務或求助。社交恢復機制 (Social Recovery): 雙守護者、具備時間鎖的錢包救援機制。用戶若遺失私鑰，可透過其指定的兩位社群守護者，將身分與陰德值轉移至新錢包。輪迴稅 (Reincarnation Tax): 獨特的賽局理論設計。若帶有「負面陰德值」的用戶發起社交恢復，將面臨 1.33 倍的債務放大懲罰，藉此杜絕惡意洗白帳號的行為。無手續費交易 (Gasless): 支援 ERC-2771 元交易標準。用戶可透過 Web2 友善的登入方式（如 Google/LINE SSO）無縫參與互動，全程無需支付 Gas 費。🛠 技術架構部署網路: Base (L2)代幣標準: ERC-1155升級架構: UUPS 可升級代理合約 (Upgradeable Proxy)依賴套件: OpenZeppelin Contracts📍 合約地址測試網: Base SepoliaProxy 代理合約: 0xA633AD478072f2C1216f552f710D72B691352bc4當前狀態: V5 最終版📄 授權條款本專案採用 MIT 授權條款。
